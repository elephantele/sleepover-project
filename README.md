This game is a web-based arcade game that is similar to apple catching. The bunny is very tired and it is up to you to decide whether or not she has a good or bad dream! Use the arrow keys to move the bunny to catch dreams as they fall out of the sky. The goal of the game is to catch 150 good dreams in 2 minutes! With each good dream the bunny catches, you earn 10 points! But with every bad dream that you catch, your score will be deducted of 15 points with every catch. Good luck! Prevent the bunny from having a nightmare!
